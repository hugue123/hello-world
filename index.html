About Me

My name is Michael Xavier Hugue. I have been creating artwork and using my talent since the third grade.
As an Artist, I love to display those life messages and lessons through my artwork.
My entire collection I gave the name “My Story” because it was life changing experiences that influenced me to create my artwork.
I’ve enrolled at Miami International University of Art & Design, to pursue a Bachelor Degree in Graphic Design.
I enjoy riding my bike and traveling, networking doing artshows painting and helping others.
